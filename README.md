# Pizza-Sales-Analytics
Conducting a thorough examination of pizza sales data, we've unearthed invaluable insights into the operational dynamics of a pizza enterprise. Leveraging SQL queries, we've extracted essential Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and trends, subsequently transforming them into visually engaging interactive dashboards within Tableau and Excel. 

## Overview:
Conducting a thorough examination of pizza sales data, we've unearthed invaluable insights into the operational dynamics of a pizza enterprise. Leveraging SQL queries, we've extracted essential Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and trends, subsequently transforming them into visually engaging interactive dashboards within Tableau and Excel. This meticulous analysis provides a comprehensive view of the company's fiscal vitality, consumer tendencies, and product efficacy.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

A. Total Revenue:
Calculates the total revenue generated from pizza sales.

B. Average Order Value:
Calculates the average value of each order placed.

C. Total Pizzas Sold:
Calculates the total quantity of pizzas sold.

D. Total Orders:
Calculates the total number of orders placed.

E. Average Pizzas Per Order:
Calculates the average number of pizzas in each order.
Trends Analysis

F. Daily Trend for Total Orders:
Displays the total number of orders placed each day of the week, allowing you to identify trends in order patterns over the days.

G. Hourly Trend for Orders:
Displays the total number of orders placed in each hour of the day, enabling you to understand peak hours of order placement.
Sales Distribution

H. % of Sales by Pizza Category:
Calculates the percentage of total sales revenue contributed by each pizza category, helping you identify revenue-driving categories.

I. % of Sales by Pizza Size:
Computes the percentage of total sales revenue generated by each pizza size, offering insights into size popularity.
Detailed Analysis

J. Total Pizzas Sold by Pizza Category:
Provides the total quantity of pizzas sold for each pizza category within a specific month (e.g., February), facilitating the analysis of sales patterns for different categories.

K. Top 5 Best Sellers by Total Pizzas Sold:
Lists the top 5 best-selling pizzas based on the total quantity sold, guiding inventory management and marketing strategies.

L. Bottom 5 Best Sellers by Total Pizzas Sold:
Lists the bottom 5 pizzas based on total quantity sold, enabling the identification of less popular items and informed decision-making.
